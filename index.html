Chocolate Fudge

Description

This chocolate fudge dessert features a rich and indulgent combination of layers, including a buttery base, smooth caramel center, and a glossy chocolate topping.

Perfect for satisfying your sweet tooth, this treat is both visually appealing and incredibly delicious.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup butter
  • 2 cups sugar
  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • 2 cups chocolate chips
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract

Steps

  1. Melt butter and sugar together in a saucepan.
  2. Add cream and stir until smooth.
  3. Mix in chocolate until melted.
  4. Pour into a pan and let cool.
